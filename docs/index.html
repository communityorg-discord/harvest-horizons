Harvest & Horizons

Farm · Explore · Thrive

A cozy farming adventure where every season brings new life, and beyond the forest lies discovery. Restore an abandoned farm, help the people of Greenfield Valley, and uncover the Wizard's secret.

▶ Play in browser Learn more
Early prototype. Phase 1 is a walking sandbox with the core HUD wired up — farming, shops, quests, and the wider world land in upcoming phases.

A letter arrives

"The valley needs a steady hand, and the soil remembers those who care for it. Come to Greenfield. The old farm is yours, if you'll have it. There is more here than fields and fences — but those things will keep, for now."

— The Wizard

What you'll do

🌱

Farm & Raise

Plant seasonal crops, raise twelve kinds of animals, and build your homestead from a starter shack to a five-tier estate.

🏘️

Help the Town

Meet nine townsfolk with their own personalities, likes, and stories. Build friendships, give gifts, and complete their requests.

⚔️

Explore & Adventure

Chop the forest, mine the deeps, fish the coast, and face stronger foes as you press deeper into Greenfield Valley.

Six ranks to Guardian

Earn your place in the valley. Each rank unlocks new areas, quests, and trust from the townsfolk.

  1. INew Arrival
  2. IILocal Helper
  3. IIITrusted Farmer
  4. IVTown Restorer
  5. VValley Hero
  6. VIChosen Guardian
